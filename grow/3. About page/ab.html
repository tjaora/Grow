about

There comes a point in our life, when for some reason, we feel frustrated, hopeless, demotivated, and clueless. We feel unworthy and undeserving. We struggle to find the true purpose of our life.
During that tough time, we fail to find a friend who understands and listens to our problem. We, alone, try to start everything all over again,but fail to follow a proper guideline.
Hence, we get distracted, give up on life, suffer alone, develop a low self-esteem, start living an unordered and unhealthy life.

GROW app is here to remind us that, every human has unbelievable potential to achieve greatness.
It helps to get us back on the right track, builds a healthy and productive life style.

The app mainly contains 2 sections:
(1) Growth: This section takes care of user's physical, mental, and personal health.
(2) Tools: This section provides a lot useful tools for the users.

Growth

1. Physical

This section helps you to improve your:
1. Health
2. Diet
3. Sleep

2. Mental

This section takes care of your mental health.

3. Personal

This section helps you to make you understand yourself better.

Tools

1. To-do list

To accomplish your daily activities, a to-do list is a must.

2. Event Calendar

It helps to organize all your upcoming events.

3. Goals

This section helps you to achieve your life goals.

4. Notes

Here you can quickly note down your thoughts, ideas etc.

5. Diary

You can share everything with your personal diary.

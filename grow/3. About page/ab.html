About

There comes a point in our life, when for some reason, we feel frustrated, hopeless, demotivated, and clueless. We feel unworthy and undeserving. We struggle to find the true purpose of our life.

During that tough time, we fail to find a friend who understands and listens to our problem. We, alone, try to start everything all over again, but fail to follow a proper guideline.

Hence, we get distracted, give up on life, suffer alone, develop a low self-esteem, and start living an unordered and unhealthy life.

GROW app is here to remind us that every human has unbelievable potential to achieve greatness. It helps to get us back on the right track and builds a healthy, productive lifestyle.

The app mainly contains 2 sections:
(1) Growth: Takes care of the user's physical, mental, and personal health.
(2) Tools: Provides useful tools for day-to-day productivity.
